How to play

Connect the keywords using exactly six words from the word list.

For example, to connect GREEN and MONEY:

  • GREEN
  • APPLE
  • JACK
  • BLACK
  • EYE
  • CONTACT
  • PAPER
  • MONEY

Each pair of adjacent words should form a common phrase or compound word:

  • Green apple
  • Applejack
  • Jack Black
  • Black eye
  • Eye contact
  • Contact paper
  • Paper money

Proper nouns (like "Jack Black") are acceptable!

Tips

  • Each puzzle has one correct solution.
  • Work from both the front and the back. Try meeting in the middle!
  • Watch out for words that are interchangeable. For example, DROP and BACK may become "Drop back" or "Backdrop."
